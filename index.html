Getting Started

1 - Download the librairies and stylesheets

jQuery >= 1.8.3 and FSBGalery

2 - Define a wrapper

Give it any jQuery selector of your choice

3 - Insert the scrips

The librairies and an initializer script

4 - Configurate your galery

Add the link of fsbgalery.css stylesheet, or feel free to integrate it yourself :)

	<section id='fsb-galery-wrapper'></section>

	
	<script type='text/javascript' src='assets/js/jquery-1.8.3.min.js'></script>
	<script type='text/javascript' src='assets/js/fsb-galery-1.0.0.js'></script>
	<script type='text/javascript'>
		$('#fsb-galery-wrapper').FSBGalery({
			// Choose your plugin configuration here
		});
	</script>
